About Me

I operate at the intersection of technical product leadership, platform strategy, and regulated healthcare systems. My work focuses on building and scaling healthcare SaaS platforms that enable reliable clinical decisions, strong governance, and measurable business outcomes. I own and shape multi-product platforms across electronic patient records, provider and clinical data management, interoperability, quality and compliance systems, analytics, and AI-enabled decision support. Rather than optimizing for feature delivery, I focus on designing product systems—clear strategy, decision frameworks, and metrics that allow teams to execute effectively at scale. I partner closely with engineering, data, clinical, and executive stakeholders to balance speed with safety, innovation with compliance, and short-term delivery with long-term platform health. My leadership philosophy centers on judgment, clarity, and outcome ownership in complex, regulated environments.
